Growing the leaders of tomorrow

Founded in 1965, UCI has accomplished a plethora of achievements from being a member of the prestigious association of American Universities to producing 3 Nobel laureates. UCI is known for it's academic excellence and it's pioneering efforts in creating leaders. UCI has more than 37,000 students and offers 255 degree programs.
